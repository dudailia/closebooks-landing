How it works

From raw bank data to close-ready books in 4 steps

CloseBooks plugs into your existing workflow — QuickBooks, Xero, or CSV uploads

1

Connect or upload

Link your clients' bank accounts via Plaid or upload CSV/PDF statements. We normalize every format automatically.

2

AI categorizes

Our AI maps each transaction to your client's specific Chart of Accounts — learning your firm's patterns over time. 85-95% are auto-matched.

3

Auto-reconcile

Bank transactions are matched against your GL entries. Exceptions and anomalies are flagged — you review only what matters.

4

Review and close

Approve the AI's work in a clean dashboard. Generate P&L, balance sheet, and close checklist. Push back to QuickBooks or Xero.
